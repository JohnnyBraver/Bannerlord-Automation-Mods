[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Equipment Manager is an automated gear optimization and acquisition tool. It handles companion hand-me-downs, civilian loadout priorities, and can request exact high-tier or stealth armor upgrades from merchant inventories based on your customizable budget and spend-mode settings.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (specifically inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "EquipmentManager" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Equipment Manager" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Multi-Pass Headless Auto-Equip: Automatically optimizes gear for you and your companions after battle and before trading.
- Gear Cascades (Hand-Me-Downs): Unequipped upgrades are passed down to companions in party list order.
- Priority Loadouts: Choose custom priority layouts between Sneaking, Civilian, and Combat gear.
- Auto-Buy Upgrades: Requests exact premium armor or sneaking-item upgrades from merchants when your gold and reserve settings allow it.
- Spend Modes: Choose how top armor and stealth gear upgrade requests compete with other automation needs.
- Anti-Sell Safety Lock: Keeps unequipped items locked from being sold if they represent an upgrade/sidegrade for any companion.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Settlement Automation Core
- Harmony
- MCM[/color][/size]
