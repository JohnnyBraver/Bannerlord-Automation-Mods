[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Trade Optimizer is an automated trade engine. It automatically buys and sells commodities when you enter settlements, executing profitable transactions based on average prices, category policies, budget protection, and cargo limits. Party restocking needs are handled by Party Manager.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "TradeOptimizer" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Trade Optimizer" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Auto-Trade on Entry: Seamless trade execution whenever entering town/village gates.
- Dynamic Profit Calculations: Sells and buys based on average local or global price margins.
- Budget & Cargo Protection: Restricts spending based on wages protection, safety gold, and party carry capacity.
- Category Policies: Configure whether food, livestock, and mounts may be bought, sold, both, or ignored as trade goods.
- Optional Stealth Buy: Can buy "Blackened" stealth gear when explicitly enabled, outside normal commodity restrictions.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Settlement Automation Core
- Harmony
- MCM[/color][/size]
