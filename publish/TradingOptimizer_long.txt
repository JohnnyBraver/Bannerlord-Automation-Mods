[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Trading Optimizer is an automated trade engine. It automatically buys and sells commodities when you enter settlements, executing profitable transactions based on average prices. It also satisfies party logistics goals (variety food, mount upgrades) while respecting market price thresholds.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "TradingOptimizer" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Trading Optimizer" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Auto-Trade on Entry: Seamless trade execution whenever entering town/village gates.
- Dynamic Profit Calculations: Sells and buys based on average local or global price margins.
- Budget & Cargo Protection: Restricts spending based on wages protection, safety gold, and party carry capacity.
- Logistics Fulfillment: Auto-buys food supplies for steward XP and speed mounts for infantry, using throttle multipliers to avoid overpaying.
- Stealth Arbitrage: Prioritizes and auto-purchases "Blackened" stealth gear from merchants, bypassing normal trade checks.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Settlement Automation Core
- Harmony
- MCM[/color][/size]
