[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Party Manager is an all-in-one party management and automation tool. It automates troop recruitment from notables and taverns, handles prisoner keeping, ransoming, and dungeon donations, and owns party needs such as food restocking, food variety, riding mounts, and mounts/herding cleanup.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "PartyManager" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Party Manager" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Master Toggle: Disable Party Manager entirely without unloading the module.
- Role & Culture Filters: Recruit exactly the troops you want based on frontline, shock infantry, cavalry, ranged, or specific culture tags.
- Grouped Hiring: Notable volunteers and tavern mercenaries are planned together, then Core applies shared gold and party-space limits.
- Hire Order Setting: Sort allowed recruits by best candidate, best value, cheapest first, volunteers first, or mercenaries first.
- Consolidated Prisoner Management: Plans dungeon donation and tavern ransom together after recruitment, so prisoner capacity reflects the final party size.
- Post-Automation Alerts: Real-time warnings when your party is near prisoner capacity or single stacks grow too large.
- Party Needs: Maintains critical food days, requests a variety of known food types, fills the total food buffer, and buys riding mounts for infantry.
- Mounts & Herding: Can preserve riding mounts for foot troops, prefer selling pack animals before riding mounts, warn on herding/cargo slowdowns, and use category-average mount pricing.
- Spend Modes: Choose how food and mount requests compete with other automation requests, from Critical through Luxury.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Save compatibility[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Safe to add to new or existing campaigns. This mod does not write custom save data. It only performs normal game actions when automation runs, such as recruiting troops, ransoming prisoners, donating prisoners, and requesting food or mounts, so those in-game results will naturally remain in your save.[/color][/size]
