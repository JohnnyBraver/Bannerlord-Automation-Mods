[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Party Manager is an all-in-one party management and automation tool. It automates troop recruitment from town and village notables (supporting detailed culture and role filters), handles prisoner keeping, ransoming, and donations, and owns party logistics needs such as food restocking, food variety, and riding mounts.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "PartyManager" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Party Manager" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Role & Culture Filters: Recruit exactly the troops you want based on shields, shock infantry, cavalry, or specific culture tags.
- Dropdown Recruitment Policies: Explicit policies for regular, noble, and mercenary recruitment (None, MatchFilters, Any, AnyIgnoreTier).
- Consolidated Prisoner Management: Policies to ransom all, keep all, or keep selected prisoners matching your recruitment filters. Supports minor faction keep policies and recruitment policy bypasses.
- Post-Automation Alerts: Real-time warnings when your party is near prisoner capacity or single stacks grow too large.
- Party Needs: Maintains critical food days, requests a variety of known food types, fills the total food buffer, and buys riding mounts for infantry.
- Spend Modes: Choose how food and mount requests compete with other automation requests, from Critical through Luxury.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Settlement Automation Core
- Harmony
- MCM[/color][/size]
