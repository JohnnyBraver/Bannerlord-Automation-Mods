[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Fief Manager is a settlement management automation tool. It handles building queues, project prioritization, and constructs buildings automatically. It also automatically deposits denars into town and castle reserves to boost construction speed while protecting your player budget.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Installation instructions[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]1. Extract the downloaded archive into your Mount & Blade II Bannerlord game directory (specifically inside the Modules folder).
2. Ensure the "FiefManager" folder is present under Modules.
3. Enable "Fief Manager" in the game launcher.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Main features[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]- Master Toggle: Disable Fief Manager entirely without unloading the module.
- Auto Build Queue: Automatically fills empty queue slots up to a configurable project count.
- Building Category Priority: Choose whether to prioritize balanced/default order, military projects, or economic projects.
- Upgrade Sorting: Choose default order, lowest level first, cheapest first, or most expensive first within the selected priority.
- Auto Project Boosting: Automatically deposits gold to boost project construction speed when useful.
- Budget Protection: Configures thresholds to never deposit gold if your player balance is low.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Save compatibility[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Safe to add to new or existing campaigns. This mod does not write custom save data. It only performs normal game settlement actions when automation runs, such as choosing construction projects or depositing gold into reserves, so those in-game results will naturally remain in your save.[/color][/size]
